Venner

"Ben took our initial designs and really turned them up to the next level. We were very happy with the outcome."

Joe Attard-Owen, Co-Founder of Venner

Brief

Venner is a social media startup, designed to help people find new friends within big cities. It integrates a match-making function with an automated event planner, and has a particularly focus on travellers and young professionals.

Some placeholder designs had been put in place for the app's website, but these needed to be improved in order to appeal to new users after launch.

The brief required the following for the website:

  • Refine the brand imagery and messaging
  • Effectively showcase the app's functions to new users
  • Clarify call to actions and make them very clear

Delivery

Working closely with Venner's developers, we decided to show the users the calendar function, utilising their location to create a sense of fomo.

The branding of the site was updated to include stock photography over illustrations, as the core selling point of the app is the people you get to meet through using it. The brand's voice was also accentuated to provide more personality to the app.

The colour scheme and content was updated to be more vibrant and exciting, whilst the explanation of the app was streamlined to enable fast comprehension.
